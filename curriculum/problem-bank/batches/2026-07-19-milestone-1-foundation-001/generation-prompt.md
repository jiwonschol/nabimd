# Milestone 1 foundation seed materialization

Materialize the twenty committed schema-v2 seed problems into one immutable build-time batch. Preserve each authored teaching goal, grammar-only match checks, editorial checks, and fixture expectation. Do not generate learner-facing prose, evaluate answers with an AI model, create reviewer verdicts, or make editorial decisions.

Run every committed seed fixture through the repository's real `normalizeProblem` and `evaluateProblem` implementation. Bind the resulting verification evidence and frozen review manifest to the explicit engine-contract policy. Keep runtime publication empty until two declared-independent reviews and a separate editorial decision exist for every accepted candidate.
