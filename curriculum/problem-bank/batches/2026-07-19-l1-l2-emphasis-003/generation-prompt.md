# Build-time generation brief: Level 1–2 bold emphasis expansion

Generate exactly 24 schema-v2 `standard` Markdown exercises for Nabi Markdown:
12 at Level 1 and 12 at Level 2. This is an immutable, review-gated batch for
Issue #9, not runtime AI output.

Every exercise asks the learner to make at least one short phrase bold. Grade
the parsed Markdown `strong` structure only. Never compare the learner's words
with the Goal, protected content, vocabulary terms, capitalization, spelling,
punctuation, meaning, document length, or technical truth. Both `**words**` and
`__words__` are valid standard Markdown and must be Matched. Bold nested in or
around a link is also real bold syntax. Italic, unclosed markers, escaped
markers, raw HTML, and markers inside inline code do not satisfy the exercise.

Multiple bold spans remain Matched. They may produce the nonblocking
`keep-bold-focused` Review because a short beginner note reads more clearly
with one emphasized point, but Review can never revoke Matched.

Level 1 uses one-to-three-word familiar labels and noun-like phrases, visible
guidance, `introduce` mode, and the `everyday` vocabulary profile. Level 2 uses
short familiar actions and routines, request-only Help, `recall` mode, and the
`everyday-recall` profile. Avoid specialist, corporate, brand, quotation,
lyric, and culture-dependent language. Reusing selected frozen vocabulary is
allowed, but its old exact-prose instructions are not.

For each candidate, provide nine deterministic fixtures: canonical, unrelated
bold prose, changed case/spelling, equivalent underscore-strong syntax, plain
text, an unclosed delimiter, italic-only syntax, markers inside inline code,
and multiple bold spans with the optional Review. Bind every candidate and
fixture to batch `2026-07-19-l1-l2-emphasis-003`, curriculum version
`2026-07-19`, and revision 1.

Do not web crawl. Do not publish any candidate until real-engine verification,
two independent reviews, and a separate eight-dimension editorial acceptance
all pass against the same frozen manifest.
